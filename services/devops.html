DevOps

If you’ve considered custom software for your organization, you’ve probably heard the term DevOps thrown around. Yet, if you ask a hundred people you’ll get a hundred and one different definitions. We have a unique perspective about how to effectively build solutions. To Aramaze, DevOps refers to the team, tools, and practices behind the design, development, deployment, release, and maintenance of your software. Our DevOps team, made up of world-class business analysts, software engineers, and developers, is ready to partner with your organization for the full lifecycle of your next game-changing software solutions and applications.

We leverage our experience and domain knowledge to propose the best set of services that can successfully achieve your goals. From assessment and consulting to actual implementation, we employ the most comprehensive solutions possible to advance your integration and release process.

Cloud adoption significantly accelerates a business’ response to modern challenges while optimizing costs. DevOps creates an agile and collaborative environment that reduces development cycle times and enables teams to focus more on driving value. Companies that combine these workflows experience a dramatic reduction in testing and deployment costs. Aramaze will help you save money on infrastructure maintenance with cutting-edge DevOps services that include:
