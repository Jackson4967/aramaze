Data and Analytics

Aramaze understands that data is at the core of any digital enterprise.. This data in many cases represents key business assets and intellectual property (IP). Data Management is the essential practice of storing, consuming, and maintaining core company IP the right way. Every enterprise has its own unique requirements for storing and managing data and trying to create a single data solution for everyone never works. Therefore, we carefully choose the approach for each specific project, based on its nature and requirements. Aramaze has experience with a range of relational database management systems (RDBMS), NoSQL databases, and message-queueing software (RabbitMQ, Kafka).

We spend a lot of time researching and optimizing the use of emerging technologies such as NoSQL and Big Data. At the same time relational SQL databases are at the center of many of our projects and can often be the best tool to use. Aramaze has decades of expertise implementing solutions based on PostgreSQL and Elasticsearch.

Aramaze helps organizations with mature business intelligence processes and those at the start of their BI journeys to build business-oriented BI ecosystems and enhance their data use and management technology. . Our BI consultants solve versatile BI challenges related to industry regulations, changing software requirements, and evolving standards of enterprise analytics and reporting. We are ready to integrate MS Power BI, Tableau, or Sisense products with your system.
