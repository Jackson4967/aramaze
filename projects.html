Travel Guide

Project information

  • Category:Mobile
  • Client:Travel Guide Inc
  • Project duration:4 months
  • Project URL:App Store/Google Store

There are so many ways to explore a foreign city and get the most from your experience. Have you ever found yourself wandering around on vacation and wishing you just a had a better plan about where to go and what to do while you are there?

We all know that a city center can be a very active, busy location. But how can a traveler unlock the true character of a major city and discover highlights and places located in different parts of the city?

This mobile solution provides tourists the opportunity to uncover hidden gems in major cities around the world, and learn about each city with their hand-held AI guide. We were inspired by Hoes - a geocaching app where the user winds his way through a city by answering questions and playing games. Thus, the user navigates to well-known and lesser known attractions. In our travel adventure app if the user is within 100 feet of a landmark he will be notified about a specific challenge. The information and the challenges are presented in the form of a video, audio file, or a quiz game.

Conference Event Manager

Project information

  • Category:mobile, web applications, events planning
  • Client:Seaside Startup Summit
  • Project duration:4 months
  • Project URL:https://www.seasidestartupsummit.com/

Let’s face it - running a conference or any thousand+ people event in person or virtually without a good mobile app is practically impossible. In today's technology-driven world, customers expect a seamless mobile experience. Our client had been hosting a successful international technology conference for years and was looking to include a mobile experience for event attendees. With the many moving parts of any event, from real-time communication, to virtual maps and schedules, a mobile app was the perfect solution.

The Aramaze team collected the necessary criteria and event details and began building a mobile application. The app coordinated multiple events and news channels, and supported push notifications for Startups, Investors, Speakers, Media, Volunteers, and Guests.

The results of the event management mobile app were positive. The app increased attendee engagement by 90% with spikes in attendee feedback noting the benefits of creating and managing their own session schedules. With the user data collected from the app, event managers were able to optimize the event according to the most popular times, sessions, and speakers.
